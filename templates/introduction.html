Ayurvedic Prakriti Finder

Ayurvedic Prakriti refers to an individual's unique mind-body constitution in Ayurveda. It is determined by the balance of the three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

Understanding your Prakriti is essential because it helps you make lifestyle and dietary choices that promote overall health and well-being. It guides you in managing imbalances and preventing illnesses.
